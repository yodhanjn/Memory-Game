## ğŸ® How to Play

1. Click on any card to flip it and reveal a Greek alphabet symbol
2. Click on another card to find its matching pair
3. If the symbols match, the cards stay flipped and you score points
4. If they don't match, the cards flip back and you lose 4 points
5. Match all 10 pairs to win!
6. Don't let your score reach 0, or it's game over!
